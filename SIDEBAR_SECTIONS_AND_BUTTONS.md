## Notes

- Buttons marked with *(Admin/Master Admin only)* are only visible to users with Admin or Master Admin roles
- Buttons with *(Master Admin only)* are exclusively visible to Master Admin users
- Many subsections marked as "No items listed yet" may have items that will be populated dynamically
- All section buttons use a green background (#1DBC83) and white text
- Submenu items use a white background with orange text (#f97316)
- The sidebar has a fixed width of 154px and is positioned on the left side of the desktop interface
